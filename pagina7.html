W3.CSS Alert

Este componente es un recuadro de alerta, que puede adoptar distintas formas, colores, tamaños... Dentro de el se pueden poner otros componentes como pueden ser botones, iconos, imagenes etc.

Displaying Alerts:

En este ejemplo, se muestran los tipos de alertas que hay (Peligro, Advertencia, Realizado favorablemente e Informacion), y la manera de mostrarlas.

Danger!

Red often indicates a dangerous or negative situation.

Warning!

Yellow or orange often indicates a warning that might need attention.

Success!

Green often indicates something successful or positive.

Info!

Blue often indicates a neutral informative change or action.

Codigo de ejemplo:
<div class="w3-panel w3-red w3-margin-left w3-margin-right w3-card-16" >
<h5> Danger!</h5>
<p> Red often indicates a dangerous or negative situation.</p>
</div>
<div class="w3-panel w3-yellow w3-margin-left w3-margin-right w3-card-16">
<h5>Warning!</h5>
<p>Yellow or orange often indicates a warning that might need attention.</p>
</div>
<div class="w3-panel w3-green w3-margin-left w3-margin-right w3-card-16">
<h5>Success!</h5>
<p>Green often indicates something successful or positive.</p>
</div>
<div class="w3-panel w3-blue w3-margin-left w3-margin-right w3-card-16">
<h5>Info!</h5>
<p>Blue often indicates a neutral informative change or action.</p>
</div>
Rounded Alerts:

En este ejemplo, se muestran los tipos de alertas que hay (Peligro, Advertencia, Realizado favorablemente e Informacion), y los distintos efectos de redondeado de las esquinas.

Danger!

W3-round: Element rounded (border-radius) 4px.

Warning!

W3-round-large: Element rounded (border-radius) 8px.

Success!

W3-round-xlarge: Element rounded (border-radius) 16px.

Info!

W3-round-xxlarge: Element rounded (border-radius) 32px.

Codigo de ejemplo:
<div class="w3-panel w3-red w3-margin-left w3-margin-right w3-card-16 w3-round" >
<h5>Danger!</h5>
<p> W3-round: Element rounded (border-radius) 4px.</p>
</div>
<div class="w3-panel w3-yellow w3-margin-left w3-margin-right w3-card-16 w3-round-large">
<h5> Warning!</h5>
<p >W3-round-large: Element rounded (border-radius) 8px.</p>
</div>
<div class="w3-panel w3-green w3-margin-left w3-margin-right w3-card-16 w3-round-xlarge">
<h5>Success!</h5>
<p> W3-round-xlarge: Element rounded (border-radius) 16px.</p>
</div>
<div class="w3-panel w3-blue w3-margin-left w3-margin-right w3-card-16 w3-round-xxlarge">
<h5>Info!</h5>
<p> W3-round-xxlarge: Element rounded (border-radius) 32px.</p>
</div>
