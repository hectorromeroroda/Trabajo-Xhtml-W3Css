Modal with Alerts:

Este ejemplo representa un Modal, que al abrir-se, muestra una alerta determinada 'Danger, Warningy Success. Los mensajes de alerta suelen utilizar-se a menudo junto a un Modal.

×
Danger!

Red often indicates a dangerous or negative situation.

×
Warning!

Yellow or orange often indicates a warning that might need attention.

×
Success!

Green often indicates something successful or positive.

Codigo de ejemplo:
<button onclick=abrir('ej3a') class="w3-btn w3-margin-left w3-teal w3-ripple" style="width:30%">Danger Modal </button>
<div id="ej3a" class="w3-modal w3-animate-zoom ">
<div class="w3-modal-content ">
<div class="w3-panel w3-red w3-card-16" >
<span onclick=cerrar('ej3a') class="w3-closebtn">×</span>
<h5>Danger!</h5>
<p>Red often indicates a dangerous or negative situation.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<button onclick=abrir('ej3b') class="w3-btn w3-green w3-ripple" style="width:30%">Warning Modal</button>
<div id="ej3b" class="w3-modal w3-animate-opacity">
<div class="w3-modal-content ">
<div class="w3-panel w3-yellow w3-card-16">

<p>Yellow or orange often indicates a warning that might need attention.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
